FAQs

Our general question

These FAQs cover common questions and help potential customers understand the benefits, process, and details of switching to solar with Evargo Solar Solutions.

How does solar energy work

Solar panels capture sunlight and convert it into electricity, which can power your home or business. Any excess energy can be stored in batteries or sent back to the grid.

Will solar panels work on cloudy days or during winter?

Yes, solar panels still produce electricity on cloudy days, although at a reduced rate. Solar systems are designed to work efficiently year-round, including in winter conditions.

How much will I save by switching to solar?

Savings vary based on factors like energy consumption, local electricity rates, and system size. Typically, customers see significant reductions in their electricity bills over time.

What maintenance is required for solar panels?

Solar panels are low-maintenance, requiring only occasional cleaning and routine checks to ensure optimal performance. We offer maintenance services to keep your system running smoothly.
